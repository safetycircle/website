The SafetyCircle team are proven specialists in WHS consulting and training.

They are coaches who set high standards and then enable teams and individuals to reach those standards. The SafetyCircle team is committed to injury and illness prevention and makes this possible for clients. They work hard as innovators and simplifiers looking to make sense and make a difference in a field that is often dogged by paperwork overload, fear-mongering, zealotry and complacency.

Each member of the SafetyCircle team possesses a rare skill set and level of experience required to pass a most rigorous selection criteria - which makes becoming a SafetyCircle trainer a difficult and extraordinary qualification that does not suit most trainers or consultants. The company appreciates that cultural change work is a steep challenge requiring only the best trainers to lead the programs.

Michael is a founding member of the IPM Safety team, he is a qualified Physiotherapist working as a WHS Consultant with over 22 years’ experience in Work Health and Safety. Martyn’s primary and passionate interest is in making a difference to work health and safety so that everyone stays safe and well every day. In 2005, with co-Director Michael Roberts, Martyn created the SafetyCircle concept and has been its most powerful advocate ever since. Martyn has over 25 years’ experience in occupational health, safety and environment in Tasmania working in manufacturing and heavy industries as well as the Tasmanian Government. Martyn has more than 15 years’ experience consulting in WHS, Occupational Hygiene and Training, providing a broad range of WHS services to organisations across Australia and New Zealand. Martyn also holds Certificate IV in Training and Assessment.
